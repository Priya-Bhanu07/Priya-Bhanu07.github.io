## 📄 STEP 1: Create Resume PDF (5 minutes)

### Option A: Use resume.html (Easiest)

1. **Open the resume file** (already opened in your browser):
   - File: `/home/bhanup/Main/Projects/portfolio/assets/resume.html`
   - Or double-click: `assets/resume.html`

2. **Print to PDF**:
   - Press `Ctrl + P`
   - Destination: **"Save as PDF"**
   - Margins: **None** or **Minimum**
   - Background graphics: **Enabled** ✓
   - Click **Save**
   - Save as: `resume.pdf` in the `assets/` folder

3. **Done!** Your resume is ready.

### Option B: Use Your Existing Resume

If you have a resume already:
